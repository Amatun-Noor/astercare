about us

Who We Are

Aster Care home is a licensed personal care home for seniors where comfort mingles with affordable budget. It is more than just a place to live. Our residents truly become part of our family. We always provide need base personalized care to our elderly citizen by treating them as if they were our own family. We know everyone’s needs are different, so our care plans are uniquely tailored to each individual.

We emphasis on combined team effort. The team comprises management, our staff and most importantly the family of our resident. We are dedicated to provide the highest quality senior care with dignity, compassion and peace of mind for their families. We’re not just any place to live; we’re the perfect place to call home!
